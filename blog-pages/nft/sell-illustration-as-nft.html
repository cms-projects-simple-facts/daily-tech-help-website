How to Register a Photo/Painting/Digital Illustration as NFT

Visual Guide

1. Pick a Blockchain

Choose where your NFT will live: Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, or Tezos.

2. Set Up a Crypto Wallet

Create a wallet like MetaMask, Phantom, or Temple to store your crypto and NFTs.

3. Fund Your Wallet

Buy some cryptocurrency (ETH, MATIC, SOL, XTZ) on an exchange and transfer it to your wallet to cover minting fees.

4. Choose an NFT Marketplace

Pick a platform like OpenSea, Magic Eden, or Objkt to mint and sell your NFT.

5. Mint Your NFT

Upload your artwork, fill in the details (title, description, properties), and mint it onto the blockchain.

6. List for Sale (Optional)

If you want to sell it, set a price or auction terms and list it for sale on your chosen marketplace.
